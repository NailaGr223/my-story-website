Naila's Goals in Tech ✈️💚

My goals in tech are very clear. I am using my Computer Science degree as a launchpad to reach Aerospace Engineering.
Right now I am stacking every skill I can get: CCNA networking, Python, data science, cybersecurity and full-stack development. All of it is training me to work on real aerospace systems one day.

In the long term I want to design safer avionics and intelligent drone solutions that can support disaster response, port logistics and coastal monitoring in places like Mombasa. I want to help build the kind of aviation infrastructure that connects Kenya better and creates real opportunities for our people.

But I do not want to stop at building technology. I want to open doors. I want to create mentorship programs so more young women from the coast can enter tech and aerospace fields. I want to show girls that their dreams do not have to stay dreams. They can build code, flight systems and entire careers.

Tech is not just my future. It is the tool I am using to make sure no other girl feels stuck the way I sometimes have. I am not here to follow the path. I am here to build a new one and bring as many of us along as I can.
